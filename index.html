Our Services

We build campaigns that fuse creativity and strategy. Each service we offer is designed to capture attention and deliver measurable results.

"I am convinced that about one‑half the money I spend for advertising is wasted, but I have never been able to decide which half."

— John Wanamaker

This famous remark highlights why we obsess over measurement. Without data, it's impossible to know which half of your marketing budget is working. We continually test and analyze our campaigns to ensure your investment drives returns, not waste.

"If it doesn’t sell, it isn’t creative."

— David Ogilvy

Ogilvy reminds us that marketing’s ultimate goal is to drive sales. A campaign can be clever or beautiful, but if it doesn’t convert, it fails its purpose. We balance artistry with performance to ensure creativity serves your bottom line.

"Imagination is more important than knowledge. Knowledge is limited. Imagination encircles the world."

— Albert Einstein

Einstein’s insight underscores our approach to ideation. We aren’t constrained by conventional wisdom; instead we explore imaginative concepts that stretch beyond the obvious. By thinking creatively, we uncover unique ways to connect with your audience.

About BeBold

We are a boutique marketing agency on a mission to make marketing smarter, more creative and more human.

Who We Are

Founded in Bergen, Norway, BeBold brings together strategists, designers and analysts who share a passion for out‑of‑the‑box thinking. We believe that marketing should not only look good – it should work hard. Our team is small by design, allowing us to collaborate closely with our clients and remain agile in a rapidly evolving landscape.

Whether we’re orchestrating a guerrilla stunt, building an interactive tool or optimising conversion funnels, we always focus on delivering measurable results. We stay curious, creative and accountable.

Our Values

  • Curiosity: We explore new mediums, technologies and ideas to find unconventional solutions.
  • Integrity: We make decisions based on data and honesty, delivering transparency and clear value for every spend.
  • Collaboration: We work as an extension of your team, embracing your vision and enhancing it with our expertise.
  • Impact: We judge success by the outcomes we create – increased brand equity, customer engagement and revenue growth.

Meet the Founder

BeBold was founded by Mathias Hermanrud, a marketer with a knack for blending creativity and analytics. After years working across industries, Mathias recognised that many campaigns were either imaginative but unaccountable or data‑driven but uninspired. His vision for BeBold is to bridge that divide – to prove that artistry and performance are not mutually exclusive.

Mathias leads the team’s strategic direction, champions experimentation and ensures that every campaign we launch has a clear hypothesis and success metric. When he’s not ideating, he’s studying the latest research and thought leadership to keep BeBold at the forefront of modern marketing.
